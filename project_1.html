In recent years, AI has had a profound impact on artistic creation, and through various generative methods, it has changed the current understanding and interpretation of images. We want to create an interactive piece around this theme to highlight the impact of AI on images. My role primarily revolves around creating design concepts and providing technical guidance.

The work, titled Heidegger's Heaps Brocade and Ash, reflects on Heidegger's discourse regarding the relationship between technology, humanity, and nature, culminating in the creation of an artistic work in the form of Heaps of Brocade and Ashes.

In his book The Question Concerning Technology, Heidegger proposed that technology is a generative framework that constrains both humans and nature. Humans cannot prevent the progress of technology, and this will eventually result in humanity losing its connection to nature.

However, in the face of this negative fate, humans possess an optimistic nature that confronts it‚Äîa capacity to turn broken things into beauty. Our artwork aims to showcase the way AI 'disrupts' images, not just in terms of their form, but also their content. We handpicked some snippets of classic images, and let an AI program have a go at them. After several iterations, these images started to lose their initial meaning. We like to call this process AI PATINA. We designed two types of AI iterative methods for the images and wrote more than ten image processing programs to create complex interrelationships between them, forming a visual presentation similar to ‚ÄòHeaps of Brocade and Ashes.‚Äô

AI patina & image processing
Classic images have long played the role of witnesses in human civilization, but with the advancement of image generation and processing technologies, images accumulate in large quantities, undergo mutation through network dissemination, and form a digital and electronic patina effect. During this process of change, the images themselves are also damaged.

To highlight the role of humans within the technological framework, we've added an interactive element. The artwork uses somatosensory technology, causing nearby images to shift as viewers move past. These images change in real-time with the viewers' actions, as if they're interacting directly, showing the art's acute awareness of the viewer's presence. Plus, viewers can engage in gesture interaction by clicking on the screen, which demonstrates the human capacity for aesthetic discernment and creativity.

Interaction Diagram
The artwork's information is dynamically displayed on the left. When viewers are within 200cm of the screen, approaching the picture triggers a somatosensory interaction that blends the images, and finger clicks on the screen trigger a screenshot gesture interaction. On the right is the gallery, where the artworks created by the audience will be displayed.

'Heidegger‚Äôs Heaps Brocade and Ash' is a philosophical art piece that explores the ties between technology, humans, and nature. It uses AI to reinterpret classic imagery, resulting in artworks full of historical value and energy. The art piece, a mix of fragmentation and rebirth, celebrates human positivity, life, and heritage. In our fast-paced tech era, it's not just about progressing but also about coexisting with nature. This work aims to inspire more to preserve our beautiful world.
