Light Up Tsinghua Campus

UX / Visual Design

Producer: Lan Min, Zhang Zijian
Designer: Wang Yifan
Planner: Guo Jinlong
April, 2021

April 25, 2021, marked the 110th anniversary of Tsinghua University. To commemorate this, I designed a school history quiz mini-program named Light Up Tsinghua Campus. This mini-program shone brightly during the anniversary celebrations and was loved by all of us.ğŸ˜

In the initial planning of the project, we aimed to weave together the school's history through various threads by designing a mini-program about the history of Tsinghua University, to enable people to get acquainted with Tsinghua's past. However, we wanted this project to be more engaging (not many people would enjoy browsing through a timeline of historical events). As a result, we came up with the idea of incorporating quizzes, adding an interactive element to the process to deepen the participants' impression.
