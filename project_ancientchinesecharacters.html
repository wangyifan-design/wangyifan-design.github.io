Living Jiagu

Designer: Wang Yifan
Client: Google Art & Culture
Supervisor: Chen Nan
September, 2019

In 2019, I participated in the "Living Jiagu" project under the guidance of Professor Chen Nan.
By using Oracle characters, a vibrant and lively text ecosystem was built. Visitors can not only learn the writing method of Oracle characters through this project, but also witness their own Oracle characters vividly jumping on the digital screen, becoming a part of the world of characters.
This project uses a stroke matching algorithm, which can quickly recognize the Oracle characters handwritten by the audience and judge whether they meet the standard.
The "Living Jiagu" project greatly enhances the interactive experience of visitors through artificial intelligence, making the learning and experience of ancient characters more lively and interesting.

Oracle Bone Script Red Packet

Designer: Wang Yifan
Supervisor: Chen Nan
November, 2018

This is one of the oracle bone script themed designs completed under the guidance of Professor Chen Nan, a hollow gold-printed red packet, with a total of ten styles: fortune, longevity, auspiciousness, double happiness, congratulations, spring, gratitude, good karma, good match, and reunion. The hollowed-out red packet combined with the inner gold card creates a beautiful three-dimensional effect.

Online Museum of Bird and Insect Pattern Decorations

Visual Design: Yang Hong
UX Design: Wang Yifan
May, 2020

By gathering and studying documents and atlases featuring bird and insect scripts from the Spring and Autumn and Warring States periods, along with information from museum exhibits of artifacts with these scripts, we organize and integrate educational content about them. An online platform is created to display these scripts, focusing on this historical era. The H5 design framework includes a homepage, an introduction to the scripts, research findings, a search engine, an online exhibition, and six cultural creation sections related to the scripts. Each main section also contains various subtopics.
