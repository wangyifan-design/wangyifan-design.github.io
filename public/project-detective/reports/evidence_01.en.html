Case File #0428-E

Internal Report — Confidential

Summary

On the night of September 12, a single-vehicle collision occurred on the East Bridge overpass. The vehicle, a gray sedan registered to John Quincy, was discovered partially submerged near the north guardrail.

Passenger Jane May sustained head trauma and brief retrograde amnesia; driver John Quincy was pronounced deceased at 02:47 due to internal bleeding.

Preliminary Findings

  • No signs of third-party interference were detected;
  • No abnormality was detected in the braking system;
  • CCTV footage from 02:12 to 02:18 is missing;

Conclusion

The incident is classified as a traffic accident caused by driver negligence.

Case filed under Category E (“Closed — Non-criminal”).

Addendum

Recommend further psychological evaluation of the survivor prior to releasing case data to family members.
