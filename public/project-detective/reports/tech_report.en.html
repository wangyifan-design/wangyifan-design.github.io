Case Reference: M-109

Investigation Summary

  • Vehicle collided head-on with the guardrail; airbags deployed.
  • No alcohol or drug influence detected.
  • Brake system registered abnormal hydraulic pressure loss.
  • Mechanical forensics (Report M-109) identified manual adjustment marks on the master cylinder return valve.

Conclusion

The brake anomaly likely caused loss of control.

Evidence indicates deliberate tampering and contamination of the hydraulic system.
