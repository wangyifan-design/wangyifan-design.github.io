<div class="content-block">
  ## The Challenge
  Real estate professionals work with large amounts of structured information. These systems are powerful but complex.
  Conversational AI introduces a new way to access information, but it is inherently unstructured and ambiguous. The
  key design challenge was integrating conversational AI into a data-heavy professional workflow without overwhelming users.
</div>

<div class="content-block">
  <img class="project-image" src="/img/rockhood/Platform Architecture.png" alt="Platform Architecture" />
  <p class="image-caption">Platform Architecture</p>
</div>

<div class="content-block">
  ## Challenge 1: Navigating Complex Property Data Through Conversation
  Real estate platforms contain large amounts of structured property data. Conversational AI allows users to access this
  information through natural language, but queries can vary widely in scope — from broad market comparisons to detailed
  questions about a single property. The challenge was designing a conversational system that could support these
  different levels of inquiry while keeping results clear and structured.
  <img class="project-image" src="/img/rockhood/Challenge 1.png" alt="Challenge 1: Navigating Complex Property Data Through Conversation" />
  <p class="image-caption">Conversation queries operate at multiple levels of scope.</p>
</div>

<div class="content-block">
  ## Challenge 2: Designing a Hybrid Exploration Experience
  Traditional real estate platforms rely on structured navigation such as filters, listings, and maps. Adding
  conversational AI introduces a second interaction layer. The challenge was creating a hybrid experience where
  conversation and structured browsing work together as one system rather than two separate interfaces.
  <img class="project-image" src="/img/rockhood/Challenge 2.png" alt="Challenge 2: Designing a Hybrid Exploration Experience" />
  <p class="image-caption">Conversation and browsing become one continuous exploration experience.</p>
  <img class="project-image" src="/img/rockhood/Rockhood UI.png" alt="Rockhood UI design showing conversational interface and property listings" />
</div>

<div class="content-block">
  ## Mobile Experience
  In addition to the desktop platform, a mobile experience was designed. The mobile interface focuses on quick access to
  property information while on the go. To support hands-free exploration, the mobile app introduces real-time voice
  conversation with the AI assistant. This allows users to discuss properties while browsing listings or viewing maps,
  creating a more natural interaction flow between conversation and visual information.
  <img class="project-image img-medium" src="/img/rockhood/Mobile Experience.png" alt="Mobile Experience" />
  Users can continue real-time voice conversations with the AI assistant while browsing listings, exploring the map, or
  viewing property details. This creates a seamless flow between conversation and visual exploration.
</div>

<div class="content-block">
  ## Connecting Buyers and Agents
  While the agent workspace focuses on managing complex property data, the platform also includes a mobile experience
  for home buyers. The buyer interface is designed for property discovery and seamless connection with real estate
  professionals. Users can explore listings, receive agent recommendations, and directly contact agents through phone or
  messaging. This connection creates a continuous flow between buyer inquiries and the agent workspace, helping
  professionals respond more efficiently.
</div>

<div class="content-block">
  ## Rockhood AI Design Principles
  ### Conversation First
  Users explore property information through natural language rather than complex navigation.
  ### One Hybrid System
  Conversational interaction and structured real estate data function as a unified system.
  ### AI Takes Action
  The AI assistant performs tasks such as comparison, analysis, and report generation to support professional workflows.
</div>

  ## Impact
  The platform successfully launched and is now available to real estate professionals in multiple U.S. states,
  including Washington, Arizona, and California. The system brings together conversational AI and structured real estate
